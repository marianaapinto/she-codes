My Favorite TV Series

by Mariana Costa



One of my favorite hobbies is to watch TV series. Here, I list some of my favorite!!!

    Drama Series

  • Prison Break


    Prison Break tells the story about Michael Scofield, a regular guy who plans a bank assault to get himself in jail, to architect from the inside an escape that will save his inocent brother from death row.



  • Breaking Bad


    Breaking Bad follows the journey of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher who decides to manufacture and sell methamphetamines after being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, to leave his family safe after he dies.



  • Stranger Things


    Stranger Things is the story about a boy who mysteriously disappeared in the 80â€™s and the efforts of his mother, brother and friends to find him, by facing terrible forces from an alternative dimension created due to the secret paranormal experiments of US government.



    Comedy Series

  • The Middle


    The Middle tells the daily life stories of the of a typical a lower middle-class American family with 3 children: a popular teenage who is good at sports but bad at school, an effusive hard-working but unlucky girl and a weirdo kid who loves books.



  • Community


    Community is about the peculiar and misfit students and staff of a community college, and their stories are full of meta-humour and pop culture references.



  • IT Crowd


    IT Crowd is a British sitcom that tells the misadventures of the IT support team of a big corporation.
